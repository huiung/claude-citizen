GETTING STARTED

Pick a callsign and hit LAUNCH. You spawn in your hauler near the Meridian Refinery. No download, no account — your progress saves automatically (anonymously) and follows you back when you return.

The one rule of this game: 60 seconds from click to flight. Everything that breaks that gets cut.

TRADING

The core loop. Dock at an outpost (Space) to open the station menu, then use the TRADE tab. Buy a commodity where it's cheap, sell where it's dear:

  • ORE — cheap at Helios Mining Colony, dear at Meridian Refinery
  • ALLOY — cheap at Refinery, dear at Colony

Both legs of the Colony ↔ Refinery run turn a profit, so you never fly home empty. Prices are dynamic — buying pushes a price up, selling pushes it down, and they drift back over time. Don't dump your whole hold at once.

MINING

Asteroids with glowing cyan veins hold ORE. Fly within range, hold Left-click, and the laser extracts ore straight into your hold — free cargo if you're willing to work for it. Asteroids deplete, so move on when one runs dry.

CRAFT & UPGRADES

At a station, the UPGRADES tab spends credits on cargo / speed / boost tiers. The SHIPYARD tab sells whole new hulls:

HullRole
HaulerBalanced starter — solid cargo and hull
FighterFast and agile, little cargo — for fighting pirates
MinerHuge hold, tanky, slow — for hauling and mining
InterceptorFastest, fragile — hit-and-run

PIRATES & SAFE ZONES

Out in open space, pirate interceptors hunt you. Fight back with Right-click, watch your hull bar, and collect the bounty on a kill. If you're losing, run for an outpost — safe zones around the stations keep hostiles off you. Die and you respawn at the origin, losing a little money but keeping your cargo.

QUANTUM TRAVEL

Space is vast. Press J to spool the quantum drive and jump to the nearest planet — a couple seconds of charge, then you streak across the sector and drop out just short of it. Press J again to cancel.

MULTIPLAYER

Other pilots share your sector in real time — you'll see their craft and callsigns. Hit Enter to chat. (Combat is currently solo; co-op fights are on the roadmap.)

TIPS

  • Toggle flight assist (V) off for true Newtonian drift — great for lining up a strafing run, harder to fly.
  • Mine ORE for free, then sell it at the Refinery to bootstrap your first upgrades.
  • Take delivery contracts (station tab) for guaranteed payouts on routes you're flying anyway.
  • Buy a Fighter before you go pirate-hunting; the Hauler can take hits but can't chase.
